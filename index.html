Stressor-type insight

Reactivity builds across the session rather than attenuating. Cognitive and social-evaluative stressors produce progressively poorer recovery. Suggests depleted autonomic flexibility and anticipatory load.

Questions
I already get bodywork at Soulhouse. Why do I need this?
Bodywork at Soulhouse works. The assessment reads what is happening one layer underneath it, whether your nervous system has re-learned a pattern of elevated gas pedal or weak brake, and which system is the bottleneck. Most people find the assessment makes their existing Soulhouse work even more targeted, because you know exactly which system is asking for attention.
Is this actually science, or another wellness protocol?
The method uses research-grade biofeedback equipment reading six biomarkers across four physiological systems: cardiac, respiratory, electrodermal, muscular, and prefrontal. The Default Resiliency Index is a composite score built from those readings. It is a wellness application of tools that sit on peer-reviewed physiological research. Complementary to therapy, not a replacement for it. Does not diagnose or treat any medical condition.
